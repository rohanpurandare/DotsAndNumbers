How to Play!

The objective of the game is to create a loop on the board such that each square has as many edges as the number inside the square.

To create a line, simply click between the two dots you wish to create a line between. If you want to remove a line, simply click on the line you want to remove.

When a square has the correct number of edges, it will turn green.

If you realize you want to clear the board, simply press the "Clear Lines!" button, and the board will be cleared

If you want to play a new game, press the "New Game!" button, otherwise, you will get a new game shortly after you finish
